About Me

I'm a multidisciplinary artist and game designer currently based in Berlin, Germany, working as an au pair while continuing to develop my creative practice.

Recent graduate with a Triple D* in Game Design and a Merit in Foundation Diploma in Art & Design from Birmingham. My work spans digital and traditional media, from 3D environment creation and character design to oil painting, printmaking, and large-scale installation work.

I explore the intersection of contemporary art, experimental design, and digital storytelling, drawing inspiration from video games, animation, and installation art.
